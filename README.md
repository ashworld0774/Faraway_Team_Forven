# 🚆 RailMitra

### Transforming Every Train into an Intelligent Infrastructure Monitoring Platform

> **"We didn't build another inspection train. We transformed every train into an inspection train."**

---

## 📖 Overview

RailMitra is an AI-powered Train-as-Sensor Infrastructure Monitoring System designed to make railway networks safer, smarter, and more efficient.

Instead of relying solely on periodic inspections, RailMitra transforms operational trains into intelligent infrastructure monitoring platforms. As trains travel across railway tracks, they continuously collect infrastructure health data, enabling predictive maintenance and early anomaly detection.

Our objective is not to replace railway engineers but to empower them with real-time infrastructure intelligence, helping them make faster and more informed maintenance decisions.

---

## 🌍 Vision

To create a railway ecosystem where technology works alongside human expertise, enabling railway teams to detect infrastructure risks earlier, make better decisions faster, and maintain safer railway networks.

---

## 🎯 Mission

To transform operational trains into intelligent infrastructure monitoring platforms that assist maintenance teams through continuous monitoring, predictive insights, and data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Railway infrastructure deteriorates continuously while inspections are generally performed periodically.

This creates a visibility gap where:

* Track degradation may go unnoticed
* Infrastructure aging becomes difficult to monitor
* Maintenance becomes reactive rather than predictive
* Inspection costs increase over time
* Large railway networks become challenging to manage efficiently

RailMitra addresses this challenge by enabling continuous infrastructure intelligence using trains already operating across the network.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

RailMitra converts operational trains into mobile sensing platforms capable of continuously monitoring railway infrastructure.

As trains travel normally across railway tracks, they collect infrastructure health information and transmit it to the cloud for analysis.

The AI engine analyzes this data and generates actionable maintenance insights before small issues evolve into critical failures.

## 🤖 AI / ML Workflow

1. Train collects infrastructure data.
2. Data is transmitted to the cloud.
3. Historical and real-time data are stored.
4. AI analyzes infrastructure behavior patterns.
5. Anomalies are identified.
6. Railway Health Score is generated.
7. Dashboard visualizes infrastructure condition.
8. Maintenance teams receive alerts.
